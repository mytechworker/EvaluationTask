== Plugin Setup  ==
1. Log in to your WordPress dashboard.
2. In the left-hand menu, click on "Plugins".
3. Click on "Add New".
4. You can also upload a plugin from your computer by clicking on the "Upload Plugin" button.
5. Once you've found the plugin you want to install, click on the "Install Now" button.
6. Wait for the installation to complete. Once the installation is complete, you will see a success message.
7. Click on the "Activate Plugin" button to activate the plugin.

message - It's a sub text that you want to put in your section description
button_label - It's a text message that appear on CTA button. 
button_url - It's a navigation link that you want to navigate user on click event.
